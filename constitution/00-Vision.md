# ITOS v1.0 — Vision

## What is ITOS?

Institutional Trading Operating System (ITOS) is a cloud-native, modular, auditable trading infrastructure designed to operate at institutional quality standards on minimal cloud resources.

It is not a trading bot. It is an operating system for structured, disciplined, evidence-based trading decisions — starting with paper trading, governed by strict risk rules, and supported by a full audit trail.

## Core Purpose

- Provide a structured, repeatable process for evaluating and executing trades
- Eliminate emotional and impulsive decisions through systematic rules
- Prevent overtrading through memory, fingerprinting, and cooldown engines
- Build a verified track record through paper trading before any live execution
- Give the trader a professional-grade command center on any device

## Who It Serves

A single trader or small team who wants to operate with institutional discipline:
- Evidence-based entries, not gut feelings
- Risk-first thinking before signals
- Full audit trail of every decision
- Transparent thesis tracking and invalidation
- No live trading until paper trading governance is passed

## What ITOS Is Not

- Not an automated trading bot
- Not a prediction engine
- Not a black box
- Not dependent on any desktop application in production
- Not designed for high-frequency trading

## Long-Term Vision

1. Paper trade profitably for 3+ months with documented edge
2. Pass backtest governance across multiple market regimes
3. Enable live execution only after governance approval
4. Scale to additional instruments with modular engine design
5. Maintain full cloud-native operation with zero desktop dependencies
